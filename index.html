About Shramik

Find the well trained and specific labour for your Bussiness .

shramik.co is an initiative post-coronavirus-labour-crisis in India. Indian labour, factory owners, contractors and also the government faced huge obstacles with labour-related problems due to lack of a system and unorganized labour industry. shramik.co aims to organize the Indian labour industry (unskilled and semiskilled) by bringing in major reforms in terms of efficiency, productivity and insurances.
